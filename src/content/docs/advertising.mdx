# Advertising and Sponsorships

IIoT Devices is being built to support future advertising and sponsorship inventory, especially on high-intent pages covering industrial hardware categories, protocol choices, comparison workflows, and vendor research.

## Current status

Commercial placements are currently reserved in the site architecture. Their presence as labeled placeholders does not mean paid campaigns are live across the site today.

## Intended ad-management approach

The site is structured around named placement zones tied to page context. This makes it possible to manage ad inventory centrally as the site grows instead of manually placing ads across hundreds of pages later.

## Best-fit future categories

- Industrial connectivity hardware.
- Gateways, remote I/O, and related product families.
- Protocol and interoperability tools.
- Engineering software, integrations, and industrial data platforms.
