IIoT Devices is an English-language reference site built to support industrial hardware research, device selection, and connectivity planning.

## Scope

- Device families used in industrial monitoring and control environments
- Protocol and interoperability guidance for pre-purchase evaluation
- Vendor and comparison material organized around application fit

## Audience

The intended readers are engineers, technical buyers, system designers, and teams evaluating industrial hardware for operational deployments.

## Editorial approach

The site is structured to become a durable reference library rather than a short-term news feed. Coverage is designed to favor selection logic, interoperability context, and application-level relevance.
