## How to use product-family pages

<Steps>
	1. Confirm the application and device class first.
	2. Use product-family pages to understand the real category boundary.
	3. Move into vendor analysis only after the category fit is credible.
	4. Finish with comparison pages once the shortlist is narrow enough to be useful.
</Steps>
