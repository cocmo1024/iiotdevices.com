# Industrial Gateways

Industrial gateways are one of the most durable product families in IIoT research because they often sit at the boundary between field devices, controllers, and upstream monitoring or cloud systems.

## Gateways are strongest when

- Protocol translation is central to the design.
- The site needs local buffering without the full software burden of an edge computer.
- Upstream systems need a cleaner or different data path than the field layer provides directly.

## Watch for

- Buying an overpowered edge box when a simpler gateway would reduce operational burden.
- Underestimating protocol shaping, security posture, or lifecycle support needs.
