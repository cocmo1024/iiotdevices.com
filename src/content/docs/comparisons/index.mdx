## Comparison sequence

<Steps>
	1. Lock the application and device category first.
	2. Remove options that miss non-negotiable environmental or interoperability requirements.
	3. Compare vendor depth, lifecycle fit, and rollout complexity.
	4. Re-check total operating burden before making the final call.
</Steps>
