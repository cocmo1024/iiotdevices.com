IIoT Devices may collect limited technical and operational data needed to run the site, analyze usage patterns, and maintain content quality.

## Data that may be processed

- Standard server and browser request information
- Anonymous analytics or search behavior
- Information voluntarily submitted through email

## Use of information

Data may be used for site reliability, editorial planning, abuse prevention, and understanding which reference topics are most useful to readers.

This page is a general operational notice and may be updated over time.
