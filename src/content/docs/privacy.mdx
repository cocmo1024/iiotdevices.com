# Privacy Notice

This page explains how IIoT Devices may collect and use limited information associated with site operation, analytics, correspondence, and future commercial systems.

## Information that may be processed

- Standard request and device data such as IP address, browser type, and referral source.
- Operational logs used for uptime, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.
- Aggregated analytics or search behavior.
- Information submitted voluntarily through email or direct contact.

## Why information may be used

Collected information may be used to:

- Maintain reliability and security.
- Understand which hardware and protocol topics are most useful to readers.
- Improve navigation, search, taxonomy, and editorial planning.
- Review correction requests, business messages, and partnership inquiries.

## Cookies, analytics, and future advertising

The site may use cookies, analytics tools, or related technologies to measure traffic and site performance. If ad technology, sponsorship infrastructure, or affiliate systems are introduced later, this page may be updated to reflect those additions.

## External links

This site may reference external vendors, product pages, PDFs, or other third-party resources. Those services operate independently under their own privacy policies and terms.

## Policy updates

This notice may be revised as the site grows, especially if reader accounts, expanded analytics, or ad systems are added later.
