# Machine Connectivity Retrofits

Retrofit work is where many industrial connectivity decisions become most commercially meaningful. The constraints are real, the installed base matters, and the project often depends on bridging old and new systems without breaking operations.

## Common retrofit pressures

- Legacy protocols and mixed device generations.
- Limited appetite for long shutdowns or major control redesign.
- Strong need for practical interoperability and lifecycle discipline.
