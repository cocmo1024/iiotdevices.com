# Affiliate Disclosure

IIoT Devices may in the future use affiliate links on selected pages. If that happens, the site may earn a commission when readers take qualifying actions with a third-party provider.

## Disclosure principles

- Affiliate relationships should not silently dictate editorial conclusions.
- Not every vendor or product reference will necessarily use an affiliate link.
- Comparison pages should remain useful even when no affiliate relationship exists.
